Rocket Workshops

Our rocket workshops are targeted at KS3 (11-14 years old) students, following research funded by the Economic and Social Research Council (ESRC) in the ASPIRES project. Our workshops are typically hosted at local schools, and encourage KS3 and younger to design and build their own model rockets. HarwellRocketClub certified & licensed members will be on hand to guide students, and launch their rocket on A-D Impulse motors.

Downloadable Resources

HarwellRocketClub pulls on experience & expertise of its members and inspires to pass on this knowledge to anyone seeking to get involved with the amateur rocketry community. Outreach resources, technical documents, rocket build guidelines and much more will be generated into PDF documents, and made available to the public. We are open to requests, so please get in contact with any ideas you wish to see and download.
